Facts About dogs

Dogs are highly specialized, intelligent companions domesticated over 20,000 years ago. They possess an extraordinary sense of smell up to 100,000 times more sensitive than humans, can detect diseases, and hear frequencies up to 35,000 vibrations per second. Dogs have three eyelids, communicate with over 100 facial expressions, and only sweat through their paws
